What We Deliver

Two complete divisions — physical security and cybersecurity — working together to cover every angle of your protection.

Physical Security Division

Personnel & On-Site Protection

Every guard and officer deployed by RiseUp Security goes through a thorough vetting and training process before setting foot on any client site.

Armed Security Guards

Licensed armed response for high-risk environments. Our armed officers are professionally trained, legally registered, and operate under strict protocols that prioritise client safety above all.

Unarmed Security Guards

Professional, visible deterrence for commercial properties, residential complexes, and retail environments. Trained in access control, conflict de-escalation, and emergency response.

Event Security

Crowd management, access control, and safety coordination for corporate events, public gatherings, concerts, and private functions. Scalable teams deployed based on event size and risk profile.

VIP & Executive Protection

Close personal protection for executives, dignitaries, and high-profile individuals. Our protection officers are trained in threat assessment, route planning, and close-quarters response.

Mobile Patrol Services

Regular, scheduled patrols for residential estates, industrial parks, and commercial zones. Randomised patrol routes and real-time incident reporting ensure maximum deterrence.

Access Control Management

Integrated monitoring of entry and exit points using both trained personnel and modern access control technology. Biometric and card-based systems configured and managed on-site.

Cybersecurity Division

Digital Defence & Infrastructure Security

Built with specialist staff who understand the current threat landscape. We help clients understand their risks — not just fix the damage after the fact.

Network Security Assessment

Comprehensive identification of vulnerabilities in your existing network infrastructure. We map your exposure, document findings, and deliver a prioritised remediation plan.

Firewall Configuration & Management

Setting up and maintaining robust perimeter defences. Our team configures enterprise-grade firewalls, manages ongoing rule-sets, and conducts regular penetration reviews.

Threat Detection & Monitoring

24/7 monitoring services using modern SIEM and detection tools. Real-time alerting, anomaly detection, and incident escalation protocols ensure threats are caught before they cause harm.

Data Protection & Encryption

Ensuring sensitive business and customer data is protected at rest and in transit. We implement encryption standards, data classification policies, and DLP (Data Loss Prevention) frameworks.

Security Audits & Compliance

Formal audits to ensure clients meet industry and regulatory standards — including POPIA, ISO 27001 alignment, and sector-specific requirements for government and corporate clients.

Incident Response Planning

Developing detailed response playbooks so clients are never caught off-guard. Tabletop exercises, escalation paths, and post-incident review processes all included.
